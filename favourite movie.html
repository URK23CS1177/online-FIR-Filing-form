My Favourite Movie: Inception

Introduction

Inception is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. It explores the concept of entering dreams and planting ideas.

Why I Like It

The storyline, visual effects, and background score are extraordinary. It keeps you thinking even after the movie ends.
